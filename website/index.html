Welcome to CMMS

Welcome to Cougar Mountain Middle School, home of the Falcons! Thanks to the passage of the 2016 Issaquah School District Bond, we built an incredible new middle school facility for students. On a day eagerly anticipated by students, staff and families alike, the beautiful new Cougar Mountain Middle School opened on March 1, 2022. For the first time, the student body of about 640 Falcons arrived at the campus in the Talus neighborhood.
